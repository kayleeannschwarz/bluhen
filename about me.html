let's talk about it

holistic living has always been part of my lifestyle. i've grown up with a healthy mix of homeopathic ways and western medicine, and decided to try low-tox living in march of 2020. so yes, i'm new to this. i say low-tox, because i'm still in the middle of switching all my products. i'm here to say, you really don't know the immense amount of chemicals that we've incorporated into our lives until you try and cut it out. in order to minimize both waste and financial bills, i've slowly transitioned, and man can i say it's life changing.

as someone who's always had a weird mix of health issues, when i heard about the idea of non-toxic living, i literally said why not. let's give it a chance, it'll probably be a waste of time, but maybe something will help. and man was i proven wrong. making the switch to low-tox living has changed my health, my hormones, my skin, my sleep, my migraines and so much more for the better! and i don't say that lightly. while there are many ways to make the switch, i went the essential oil route, and that's where my products, recipes and help come from.
