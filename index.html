welcome to blühen

live well. choose clean. flourish.



the purpose of this site is to educate about non-toxic living in order to encourage readers to take steps towards living a non-toxic life. it also serves as a homebase that houses a variety of information that is beneficial to those that may be beginning the non-toxic journey. whether you're looking to learn more about non-toxic living, looking for ways to make the switch or just looking for a community, this site is for you! non-toxic living is a process, it doesn't happen overnight, so utilize these tools to develop tangible ways and timelines to work through removing toxic items from your home. take a deep breath, dive in deep and get ready to flourish!





wait, but what even is non-toxic living?

non-toxic living is filling one's home and life with products that are free of harmful substances, including chemicals and poisons. many products used on the daily can cause harm, often unknowlingly. removing toxic products from one's home and routine and replacing them with organic, natural, chemically-free, environmentally-safe substitutes is living non-toxic.
